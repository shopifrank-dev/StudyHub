Questions

Before you join

Can strangers message me?
No. Messaging only works between accepted connections — there's no way for someone you haven't connected with to send you a message. You can block or unblock anyone at any time.
Is this just another social network?
No. Every part of StudyHub is built around academic collaboration — study groups, homework help, peer tutoring, and a growth record tied to your real coursework, not a general social feed.
What does Learnora (the AI assistant) cost?
Learnora is free to use. Each account gets a daily message allowance designed for regular study use — explanations, file uploads, and quick questions.
Do I need my friends to already be here?
No. Onboarding matches you with relevant classmates immediately — even with zero existing connections, you'll see real suggestions with reasons attached before you've finished setting up.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Your activity, reputation history, posts, and overview stats can all be exported at any time.
Is my activity visible to everyone?
You control your profile's visibility, and private connection notes are only ever visible to you. Department rankings show your position, not your private activity details.
